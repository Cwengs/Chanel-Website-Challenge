About Chanel

Chanel , a pioneer in fashion, has been setting trends for decades. Founded by Gabrielle "Coco" Chanel, the brand is synonymous with luxury, elegance, and timeless style. Even the plainest styles represent boldface dressing at its most chic.

From the iconic Chanel suit to the timeless Chanel No. 5 perfume, each creation embodies the spirit of modernity and innovation.

Iconic Collections




Chanel Tweed Suit


The CHANEL tweed suit was undeniably one of Gabrielle Chanel's most enduring and iconic designs, and became the defining garment of her post-war legacy. The Chanel suit revolutionized women's fashion by introducing a blend of comfort and sophistication.
You don't have to be a fashion expert to recognize the unparalleled significance of a Chanel tweed jacket. In the realm of luxury, logos typically take the lead, closely followed by accessories. However, a Chanel jacket stands apart as more than just an accessory. While the brand's iconic interlocked Cs might be subtly featured on its buttons, noticing this detail requires a close and intimate examination.




Chanel Modes


Coco Chanel set foot into the fashion industry after creating hats under the name “Chanel Modes” in 1909. Her hats were worn by well-known French actresses of the era, and helped build her reputation. Recently, at Chanel's fall 2024 show, models wore giant sun hats in pastel pink and blue shades. They are, by definition, main character hats
